How do I submit a new grievance?
To submit a new grievance, log in to your account and click on the "Submit New Grievance" button on the dashboard. Fill out the required details including the nature of your grievance, department concerned, and any supporting documents. Once submitted, you will receive a confirmation with a unique grievance ID.
How long does it take to process a grievance?
Processing time varies depending on the nature and complexity of the grievance. Simple issues might be resolved within 7-10 working days, while more complex matters may take up to 30 days. You can always check the status of your grievance on your dashboard.
Can I update or provide additional information to my submitted grievance?
Yes, you can provide additional information to your grievance. Navigate to your dashboard, find the specific grievance, and click on "View Details." There you will find an option to add comments or upload additional documents related to your grievance.
What happens if I'm not satisfied with the resolution?
If you are not satisfied with the resolution provided, you can request a review of your grievance. Go to your dashboard, locate the resolved grievance, and click on the "Request Review" button. Provide reasons why you believe the resolution is not satisfactory, and your case will be escalated for further review.
How can I check the status of my grievance?
You can check the status of your grievance by logging in to your account and going to the dashboard. All your submitted grievances will be listed there along with their current status. Click on "View Details" for more comprehensive information about a specific grievance.
